⚖️ DGC

Function: Governs.

DGC provides vision, strategic direction, mission alignment, priorities, decisions, and accountability.

📊 DIMS

Function: Manages.

DIMS organizes projects, tasks, teachings, briefings, decisions, snapshots, and operational data.

🧭 KUBERNĒSIS

Function: Visualizes and steers.

KUBERNĒSIS is the Mission Control dashboard that displays system status, projects, tasks, resume points, and active work.

⚓ ANCHOR

Function: Preserves continuity.

ANCHOR is the ChatGPT continuity mechanism used to resume work across long chats or new sessions.

💨 NESHAMAH

Function: Inspires.

NESHAMAH is the Prophetic Inspiration Engine based on Job 32:8 — the inspiration of the Almighty giving understanding.

Output: Logos, Rhema, understanding, insight, directives, declaration.

🌅 DDBB

Function: Briefs.

DDBB is the Divine Daily Bread Briefing: Scripture, KUBERNĒSIS oversight, Logos, Rhema, World Intel, Bridge Intel, Personal Intel, Confession, Directives, Tasks, Oversight, and Everyday Everything.

🛡️ SHOMER

Function: Watches.

SHOMER is the Peace & Safety Intelligence layer: geopolitical monitoring, risk barometers, watch lists, and strategic indicators.

🗣️ MARTUREŌ

Function: Witnesses.

MARTUREŌ records testimony, fulfillment, witness records, Kingdom evidence, and memorial declarations.

📚 VAULT

Function: Remembers.

VAULT stores teachings, archives, snapshots, testimony, content, family legacy, and historical records.

💡 OrEl

Function: Creates.

OrEl is the content creation engine for Principles, Perspectives, Diagnoses, Teachings, Briefings, and Prophecy/NESHAMAH materials.

🤖 DivineID

Function: Assists.

DivineID is the future Dominion1st AI assistant layer that will search, recall, recommend, summarize, and help govern work.
